Tips for Best Results

Image Quality

Use high-resolution images with good lighting for better accuracy.

Face Visibility

Ensure the face is clearly visible and not obscured by objects or shadows.

Face Size

The face should occupy a significant portion of the image for optimal detection.

Front-facing

Front-facing or slightly angled faces work best for emotion detection.

Frequently Asked Questions

What emotions can be detected?
Our system can detect five primary emotions: Happy, Sad, Angry, Surprise, and Neutral expressions.
How accurate is the emotion detection?
